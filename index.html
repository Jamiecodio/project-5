Introduction

This site is designed to talk about my career and how I ended up taking this course from Correlation One. My strengths have been writing and proofreading, and now I would like to learn how to code.

About Me

I grew up in Washington, DC, and went to college at Virginia Tech, where I experienced living in the mountains and got to know people that live and work there. After college I moved back to DC and started working for nonprofits. I then got a masters degree in Public Administration from George Mason University, a reputable school that attracts students from all over the country for its academics and being close to DC.

Brief Description

The information below gives details of my life and my career and how I ended up taking this course at Correlation One. I never thought I would end up learning how to code!

The blog post will also talk about the variety of jobs I have had and what I have learned so far in coding and my goals in making it into a career.

Blog Post

I have worked for nonprofits and public relations firms throughout my entire career since graduating from college in 2004. I enjoyed the meaningful work for the nonprofits and their missions, and I enjoyed the challenges of working in public relations. I spent over 6 years working for Qorvis Communications, and then in 2021 I was laid off due to Covid reduction.

Due to the difficulty of finding a follow up job at a nonprofit or PR firm, I accepted a job as Assistant Manager at Loyal Companion, a boutique pet supply store for dogs and cats, and went back into retail for the first time since I was still in college. I learned a lot at the pet supply store and was able to apply a lot that I had learned in obtaining a certification in Digital Marketing.

Unfortunately, Loyal Companion ending going out of business and Ihave trouble rebuilding my career since then. This is what brought me to Correlation One and sparked my interest in coding. I have learned how to incorporate headings, images, colors, and design lists, to name just a few of the skills I have picked up in this class. I hope to be able to form a career working for Amazon, one of my favorite companies that offers access to all kinds of books, movies, kitchen products, you name it Amazon has got it.
